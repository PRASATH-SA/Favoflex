Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is Favoflex?

Favoflex is an online marketplace offering customizable apparel and accessories, allowing customers to design their own products.

2. How do I place an order?

You can place an order by selecting a product, customizing it according to your preferences, and completing the checkout process.

3. Can I return customized products?

Unfortunately, customized products are non-returnable unless they are defective or damaged upon arrival.

4. How can I track my order?

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order is shipped, allowing you to track it on our courier's website.

5. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping for most products. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on location.

6. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other online payment methods for your convenience.

7. How do I contact customer support?

If you have any inquiries, please reach out to our customer support team at customercare@favoflex.com.
